From the Developers

Thank you for purchasing Warcraft III: The Frozen Throne!

Blizzard's Mac team has worked hard over many months to bring this expansion to Warcraft III to Mac gamers everywhere. Warcraft III: The Frozen Throne runs on both Mac OS 9 and Mac OS X and supports our premier matchmaking service, Battle.net, as well as network multiplayer and single player campaign modes. It also supports the latest in OpenGL technology for breathtaking 3D graphics and special effects.

Whether on Battle.net or on your local network, you'll find that we have gone to great lengths to provide for seamless gameplay between both Mac and PC players and we're shipping both versions in the same box from day one.


See you on Battle.net!

Blizzard Entertainment
